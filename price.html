Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our billing and data practices.

How do you protect my medical data?

We use bank-level AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and TLS for data in transit. We are fully DPDP '23 compliant and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel at any time through your dashboard. You will continue to have access to your Premium features until the end of your current billing period.

Do you negotiate the bills for me?

Our platform provides the intelligence, error detection, and scripts needed to negotiate effectively. Enterprise clients may opt for full-service managed negotiation as an add-on.
